# Code of Conduct

Jotty should be a welcoming project for people who want to build thoughtful software together.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent, then clarify when something is confusing.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep discussions focused on making the project better.
- Respect boundaries, identities, and lived experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissive or demeaning language
- Unwanted sexual attention
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated disruption after maintainers ask for a change in behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code. Maintainers may also limit participation for people who repeatedly make the project unsafe or unproductive.

To report a concern, open a private security advisory or contact the project owner through GitHub.
